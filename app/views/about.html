About Campus QuickStart (CQS).

CQS is a webapp to allow non-technical users to create a web presence for their on-campus clubs and organizations. Users can create a basic website for their group, link their Facebook, Twitter and Google+ pages all for free hosted on Github. And for users who would like to edit their pages directly, they are able to export their site to zip or tar formats.

WE'RE HERE TO HELP

We believe students have great ideas that could help further develop the campus community. However, for those without the technical experience, these ideas go undiscovered. This is where Campus QuickStart comes in. We'll help you develop your online presence, so you can be at ease and focus on the rest.
